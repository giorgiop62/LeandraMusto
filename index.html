About

Leandra Musto

Neapolitan at heart, in love with music, art, and the good life.

My name is Leandra Musto, I'm 36 years old, and I live in Naples, a city that runs through my veins. After spending some time away, I felt an irresistible pull that brought me back home. The connection I have with this land goes beyond words: it's the smell of the sea, the warmth of the people, the sounds of the streets. I firmly believe that if someone truly wants to challenge themselves, they should do it where the challenges are bigger and more complex. And Naples is one of those places that tests you, pushes you to improve, yet welcomes you with its energy and contradictions. In my work, I am a tour guide, and I love showing visitors the secrets and wonders of this city. I enjoy telling stories, sharing anecdotes, and seeing the amazement in the eyes of those discovering Naples' charm for the first time. But that's not all. I've always had a passion for music, art, and dance. I've studied singing, dabbled in painting, and learned various dance styles. I love expressing myself through these arts because I believe they are all different forms of communication, beauty, and freedom. My dream is to find a way to bring all these passions together in a project that can tell the story of Naples in all its nuances. I want to create events, experiences, and tours that combine music, dance, art, and history, offering participants not just a guided tour, but an emotional journey through the traditions, colors, and soul of the city. Because Naples is not just a place; it's an experience that touches the heart and deserves to be lived to the fullest.
